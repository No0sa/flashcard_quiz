# Flashcard Quiz

An interactive and visually stunning Flutter application designed to make learning fun and effective through customizable flashcards.

## 🚀 Features

- **Interactive Learning**: Study your flashcards with a smooth 3D flip animation that reveals the answer.
- **Manage Your Deck**: Easily add, edit, or delete flashcards to create your own personalized study sets.
- **Dynamic Navigation**: Navigate through your flashcards with seamless slide transitions and intuitive controls.
- **Progress Tracking**: Keep track of your progress as you move through each card in your set.
- **Premium User Experience**: Experience a beautiful, modern UI with vibrant gradients and elegant animations.
- **Real-time Updates**: Your flashcard deck updates instantly with built-in state management.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Framework**: [Flutter](https://flutter.dev/)
- **Language**: [Dart](https://dart.dev/)
- **State Management**: [Provider](https://pub.dev/packages/provider)
- **Animations**: Matrix4 Transform Animations & TickerProvider

## 📦 Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following installed:
- [Flutter SDK](https://docs.flutter.dev/get-started/install)
- [Dart SDK](https://dart.dev/get-started/sdk_setup)
- An IDE (VS Code, Android Studio, or IntelliJ)
